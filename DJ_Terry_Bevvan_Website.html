Behind the decks

Professional, prepared, and built for the room.

DJ Terry Bevvan was witnessed to house music in 1997 and started DJing in 2005. From formal wedding moments to late-night club energy, Terry brings a clean setup, a steady presence, and a music selection shaped around the people in front of him.

Every event gets planned with care: must-play songs, do-not-play lists, special announcements, timing, setup details, and the flexibility to adjust when the dance floor asks for something different.

Off-stage styleRooted in club culture, streetwear, family, and the kind of personality that makes clients feel comfortable before the first track drops.
